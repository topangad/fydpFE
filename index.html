Xylo Music

Xylo Music is a location-based, crowdsourcing music app that uses spotify to play songs of everyones choice

Share your room codes with friends, family and even strangers to build a playlist containing songs most liked by the people within a room.

Download the Android or iOs versions here



1. Create a Room and enter an optional pass phrase.
2. Share the room code with those around you.
3. Vote for your favorite songs and Downvote those you don't like.
