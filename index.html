Xylo Music

Xylo Music is a location-based, crowdsourcing music app that uses spotify to play songs in groups through the comfort of your smartphones.

Share your room codes with friends, family and even strangers to build a playlist containing songs most liked by the people within a room.

Download the Android or iOS versions here

GETTING STARTED

1. Create a Room with optional pass phrase.

2. Share the room code with those around you.

3. Vote/downvote songs.



FEATURES

Vote And Downvote Songs

Song with most votes is played next

Filter Explicit Songs

Cater to kids and adults audiences

Spotify Integration

Browse the entire Spotify Music Collection

Importing Existing Spotify Playlists

Tap the playlist name and hit save

Location Based Sharing

Guests can only join a room within 300m of the host

Easily Join Rooms

Joining rooms is easy through the passphrase
